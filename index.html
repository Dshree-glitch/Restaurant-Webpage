Our Story

Founded in 2024, Savory Bistro was born out of a passion for bringing the rich, aromatic flavors of traditional kitchens to a modern, sophisticated setting.

Every dish we serve is crafted with hand-picked spices and time-honored techniques, ensuring that every bite feels like a journey back home. Whether it's our charred Chicken Tikka or our slow-cooked Gajar ka Halwa, we celebrate the art of authentic dining.
